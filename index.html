Wash your hands with soap and water after touching animals and animal products.

When coughing and sneezing, cover your mouth and nose with a flexed elbow or tissue.

Avoid travel if you have a fever and cough.

If you have a fever, cough and difficulty breathing seek medical care early.

If you are coughing or sneezing, wear a mask and you must know how to dispose of it properly.

Eat only well-cooked food.
